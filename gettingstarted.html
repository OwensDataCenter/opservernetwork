Hello! Since you are visiting this page I am guessing that you are new to things like this or you are new to my Arduino based platform.

Let's get started.

Step 1

Download the Arduino IDE

Click Here To Download The Arduino IDE

Step 2

Get your ESP32 ready!

You will have to look for the board in the Arduino IDE.

Click Here To See If Your Board Is compatible

Step 3

Get ready as things can get a bit hard!

The Device ID is a MD5 Hash, There is a sample Device ID, It will work but a word of caution, do not keep using the sample Device ID and get one here: https://passwordsgenerator.net/md5-hash-generator/

All you need to change for right now is the WiFi SSID & Password

Look for your board on the page then copy the code and paste it in the IDE.

Open Code

Step 4

You might have a error saying 'LoRa.h: No such file or directory'

You will need to download the LoRa library at https://github.com/sandeepmistry/arduino-LoRa

If you dont know how to do this, You should search YouTube on how to do that.

If you dont get this error click Next. (Everything is fine!)

Step 5

Once the program has uploaded its time to open the serial monitor.

Wait for a max of 10 seconds, If you dont see 'Wi-Fi Connected!' in the serial monitor unplug then replug the USB cable into the ESP32 and hold the RST button and reopen the serial monitor.

If you see 'Wi-Fi Connected!' you successfully connected to your router's Wi-Fi!

Step 6

Now for the fun part!

We get to see the data, Click the button below to see the data from your device!

You should see something like this '92,dev10c7979d9309dc9ac1f478198f73d3e8,-76,2,undefined,undefined,undefined'

92 is the battery precentage & dev10c7979d9309dc9ac1f478198f73d3e8 is the Device ID & -76 is the WiFi RSSI & 2 is the boot count
